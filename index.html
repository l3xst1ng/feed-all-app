Ending Hunger, One Meal at a Time

Welcome to FeedAll, where our passion lies in eradicating hunger from our neighborhoods, one meal at a time. We firmly believe that access to wholesome, nutritious food is a fundamental human right – irrespective of one's background or circumstances.

As a community-driven non-profit, we thrive on the generous contributions and solidarity of local individuals, businesses, and organizations. Their support is the lifeblood that sustains our vital initiatives.

We invite you to rally behind our initiative – to eradicate hunger from our communities while simultaneously curbing the detrimental environmental impact of food waste. Together, we can uplift those grappling with food insecurity and pave the way for a greener, more sustainable future.

Join our cause and help nourish communities in need. Together, let's make a difference by ensuring no one in our community goes to bed hungry. Your generosity today can bring smiles, warmth, and nourishment to those in need while minimizing environmental waste.

What We Are

FeedAll is a community non-pofit organization. Together, we take good quality surplus food from right across the community and food industry and get it to needy indivuals and groups in our community.

What We Do

We believe that no good food should ever go to waste when so many go hungry. Through our Food Rescue Program, we pick up surplus, perfectly edible food from households, businesses and events, across the community. This rescued food is immediately redirected to individuals, shelters, and meal programs serving those facing food insecurity. By intercepting this nutritious fare before it can be discarded, we turn potential waste into nourishing meals while reducing environmental harm.

Getting Involved

Food Donation

Your food donations fuel our fight against hunger and waste. We gladly accept most non-perishable, unexpired food items as well as fresh foods like produce, baked goods, and perishables within 4 days of the best-by date.

Our trained food handlers ensure all donations are properly and safely redistribution to needy individuals and groups. Help us eliminate waste and hunger!

Receiving Food

If you or someone you know is experiencing food insecurity, we're here to help. FeedAll partners with local shelters, food pantries, and meal programs to get rescued food into the hands of those who need it most.

To get started receiving food assistance, please complete click on Get Food and fill out the form: After submitting the form, one of our staff members will reach out to connect you with our nearest distribution partner based on your needs.

Volunteering

Volunteers are the heartbeat of FeedAll. Join our dedicated team of Food Rescue heroes who pick up and deliver donated food to our two distribution centers.

Volunteering is an incredibly rewarding way to take direct action against hunger in our community. Contacts us today and join our volunteering team for a shift that fits your schedule.
